APPEAL BEFORE SUPREME COURT

Appeal against High Court’s order in respect of Appellate
Tribunal’s order lies with the Supreme Court in those cases,
which are certified to be fit one for appeal to the Supreme Court.

Special leave can also be granted by the Supreme Court
under Art. 136 of the constitution of India against the order of
the High Court.
